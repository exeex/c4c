Thank you for contributing to `c4c`.

- you have the legal right to submit the contribution
- the contribution is your original work, or you otherwise have the right to submit it under the project license
- you agree that your contribution will be licensed under the same license as this project: Apache License v2.0 with LLVM Exceptions

You retain ownership of your contribution. By submitting it, you allow the project and its downstream users to use, modify, distribute, and sublicense it under the project license.

## General Expectations

- keep changes focused and well-scoped
- include tests when practical
- do not submit code that you are not authorized to contribute
- clearly identify any imported or third-party code and its license terms
