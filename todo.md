### Readiness Labels

- Portable means both x86 and riscv have direct evidence for the same semantic
  fact family, prepared agreement/fallback rows remain stable, and all
  fail-closed categories above are covered without moving target policy into
  BIR.
- Partially portable means one target or one adapter surface has credible
  evidence, but parity, fail-closed coverage, or compatibility separation is
  incomplete.
- Blocked means the current matrix shows public prepared authority, missing
  x86/riscv parity, unresolved prepared compatibility, or target-policy
  contamination risk.
