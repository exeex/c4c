## Scope

This Phase D2 artifact starts from the Phase C2 retained-surface decision set.
It is analysis-only. It does not migrate consumers, delete prepared APIs,
privatize caches, open draft 155, or claim `PreparedFunctionLookups` or
`PreparedBirModule` retirement readiness.

Step 1 records the raw evidence set that later steps will use to map current
consumers and classify one-surface follow-up viability. C2 classifications are
preserved unless later D2 steps record new surface-specific evidence.

## Step 1 Closure Constraints

- Routes 3 through 7 selected adapters proved bounded route/prepared agreement
  or fail-closed validation for one selected reader or diagnostic surface; they
  did not remove public prepared helper groups, aggregate lookup fields,
  target-wrapper boundaries, printer/debug rows, oracle families, or target
  policy ownership.
- C2 accepted no bounded micro-contraction candidate. Later D2 work must start
  from retained public fallback/oracle, retained target/prepared policy,
  diagnostic/oracle replacement prerequisite, transient pass-context, or
  blocked aggregate classifications.
- Any future follow-up must be one prepared surface or one diagnostic/oracle
  row wide. It must name the route fact or retained-owner evidence, the
  fallback/oracle that remains, the target/prepared policy it refuses to move,
  and proof for positive, absent, invalid, duplicate/conflict, mismatch, and
  fallback cases as applicable.
- Draft 155, broad `PreparedFunctionLookups` retirement, broad
  `PreparedBirModule` retirement, aggregate clone/facade work, and prepared API
  deletion are not supported by this Step 1 evidence.
- Baseline, helper-oracle, diagnostic, wrapper, supported-path, and
  expected-string authority must remain non-regressive throughout D2.
